All of them: Samsung Galaxy (S, A, Note, Z Fold and Flip), Google Pixel (every model from 3a to the latest Pro), OnePlus (Nord and numbered series), Sony Xperia, Motorola (Edge, G, ThinkPhone), Nothing Phone (1, 2, 2a, 3a) and Honor.
